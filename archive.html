Reason why I like you?

You asked me once, "Why do you like me?" And I stood there silent... not because I had no answer, but because my answer had no words. You are not a reason I can explain, you are a feeling I can't escape. It's strange... how in a world so loud, your presence feels like silence— not empty, but peaceful. With you, I don't try to be better, I don't try to be different, I don't try at all... and still, I feel enough. There's something about you I can't name— like a fragrance in the air, invisible... yet impossible to ignore. I didn't choose you with logic, I didn't fall for a reason. I just... slowly became yours, without even realizing when. So if you ask me again what I saw in you... I'll still smile and say... I never saw anything. I just felt everything.
